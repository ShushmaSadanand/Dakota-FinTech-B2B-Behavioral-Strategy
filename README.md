Welcome to the FinTech Strategic Operations division of my portfolio. ---

## Case Study Executive Summary
**File:** `Transformative Customer Behaviour  - Final PPT.pdf` (Consulting Pitch Deck)

In global B2B payments, financial operators face a compounding profit drain due to hidden FX margins, delayed settlement times, and steep transaction fees (~5%) charged by legacy platforms like PayPal. This project examines how Dakota's platform is adopted from a behavioral perspective, identifies conversion bottlenecks, and builds trust-focused acquisition playbooks for high-volume cross-border SMBs.

### Behavioral Frameworks & Analytical Breakdown

* **The Core B2B Friction Bottleneck:** Isolated specific structural barriers keeping corporate targets from switching financial providers:
  * High perceived switching costs (operational inertia).
  * Systemic risk aversion regarding decentralized/blockchain-backed value settlements.
  * Opaque legacy pricing structures hiding systemic transaction bleed.
* **B2B Customer Journey Vectoring (EcoWear Use Case):** Detailed an end-to-end behavioral path tracking a cross-border apparel boutique owner:
  * **Awareness Trigger:** Realizing global margin loss via legacy payment processors.
  * **Active Consideration:** Seeking lower-friction cross-border alternatives.
  * **De-Risked Micro-Trialing:** Executing a low-risk €2,000 trial routing layer to validate instant settlement and zero-fee claims.
  * **Habit Retention Loop:** Shifting core treasury workflows onto Dakota once validated, supported by transparent monthly savings telemetry and balance yield metrics.
* **B2B Psychological Trust Engineering:** Built dual-layer value propositions converting complex technological concepts (blockchain/stablecoin mechanics) into clear, corporate utility benefits:
  * **Security Layer:** Institutional-grade custody protocols to alleviate platform risk aversion.
  * **Speed Component:** Shift from multi-day batch processing to instant, audited settlement.
  * **Yield Management:** Unlocking hidden asset value by generating returns on idle cash reserves before cross-border payouts.
* **Targeted Ad Campaign Architecture:** Developed multi-channel copy variants highlighting financial efficiency, direct fee transparency, and seamless API integrations to drive top-of-funnel customer signups.
